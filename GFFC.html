Growth Function Fight Club

How to play: Each fighter’s health and attack are growth functions of n (like n, n², n log n). As n grows to a huge hidden value, some functions dominate. For each battle, both sides’ health and attack are computed at large n, then we simulate how many attacks each side needs to defeat the other. The winner is the side that needs fewer attacks to slay its opponent (extra damage breaks ties). Your goal is to bet on the side whose growth functions will be larger at very large n.
